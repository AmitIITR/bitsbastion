NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0 Core Functions

GOVERN
Govern

Establish and monitor cybersecurity strategy, expectations, and policy

IDENTIFY
Identify

Understand assets, risks, and vulnerabilities to prioritize efforts

PROTECT
Protect

Implement safeguards to limit or contain impact of potential incidents

DETECT
Detect

Discover and analyze cybersecurity events and anomalies

RESPOND
Respond

Take action regarding detected cybersecurity incidents

RECOVER
Recover

Restore capabilities and services affected by cybersecurity incidents

NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5

Security and Privacy Controls for Information Systems and Organizations - Comprehensive control catalog for federal systems.

NIST SP 800-171 Rev 3

Protecting Controlled Unclassified Information in Nonfederal Systems - Required for defense contractors and CMMC compliance.

NIST SP 800-37 Rev 2

Risk Management Framework for Information Systems and Organizations - Process for managing security and privacy risks.

NIST SP 800-30 Rev 1

Guide for Conducting Risk Assessments - Methodology for identifying, estimating, and evaluating information security risks.

NIST SP 800-61 Rev 2

Computer Security Incident Handling Guide - Best practices for detecting, analyzing, and responding to incidents.

NIST SP 800-63 Digital Identity

Digital Identity Guidelines - Requirements for identity proofing and authentication for digital services.

NIST SP 800-88 Media Sanitization

Guidelines for Media Sanitization - Secure disposal and sanitization of media containing sensitive information.

NIST SP 800-160 Systems Security

Engineering Trustworthy Secure Systems - Systems security engineering principles and practices.

NIST SP 800-207 Zero Trust

Zero Trust Architecture - Implementation guidance for zero trust security models and architectures.
