ISO 31000 Risk Management Process

1
Establish Context

Define internal/external context, risk criteria, and risk appetite

2
Risk Identification

Identify sources of risk, events, causes, and potential consequences

3
Risk Analysis

Qualitative and quantitative analysis of likelihood and impact

4
Risk Evaluation

Compare analysis results against risk criteria to prioritize risks

5
Risk Treatment

Select and implement risk treatment options and controls

6
Monitor & Review

Continuous monitoring, review, and improvement of risk management

ISO 31000 Risk Management Principles

I

Integrated

Risk management is an integral part of all organizational activities, not a separate function.

S

Structured & Comprehensive

Systematic, structured, and timely approach to risk management at all levels of the organization.

C

Customized

Tailored to the organization's external and internal context, objectives, and risk profile.

I

Inclusive

Appropriate and timely involvement of stakeholders to ensure their views are considered.

D

Dynamic

Anticipates, detects, acknowledges, and responds to changes and events in a timely manner.

B

Best Available Information

Uses historical and current information, future expectations, and stakeholder feedback.
